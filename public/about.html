About GiveTime

Most people would happily give a few hours to a good cause. They just never hear about the ones nearby. Notices get lost in WhatsApp forwards and faded posters, so the people who want to help and the groups who need help rarely find each other.

GiveTime puts local volunteering in one plain, readable place: what the work is, who is running it, where, when, and how many helpers are still needed. Find something that fits your week and take a spot, or post your own drive and let the city find it.

How it works

Browse the opportunities page, search or filter by cause, and sign up for anything with spots left. Organisers can add new work from the post page, and edit or remove it later.

About this build

This is the full version of a four part project. The pages you see are served by a Node and Express server and talk to a MySQL database through a REST API, using fetch. Sign ups and new posts are saved in the database, so they update for everyone and survive a restart.
